Bible-based

Bible-based groups are those that use the Christian Bible as evidence for their supernatural and dogmatic claims. They shamelessly reinterpret verses of scripture and cherry-pick the parts that are convenient for them.

These groups often view mainstream Christianity with contempt and they try to convince their followers that their religion is the only path to God.

Notable bible-based groups are:

  • Jehovah's Witnesses
  • The Mormon Church
  • Unification Church
  • Jonestown
