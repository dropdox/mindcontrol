Human Potential

Human potential is a group of concepts related these senseless notions:

  • Humans only use a fraction of their brain power
  • The mind can change reality just by thinking about it
  • People can be taught how to actualize wealth and happines
  • Quantum entanglement connects people

Most peddlers of human potential dogma argue that humans can have an unlimited amount of wealth, happiness and pleasure if they develop their 'potential.'
