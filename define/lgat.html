Large-group awareness training

Large-group awareness training (LGAT) are seminars and groups that claim to be able to transform people's lives by allowing them to reach their potential.

Notable groups involved in LGAT are:

  • Leadership Dynamics
  • Mind Dynamics
  • Lifespring
  • Erhard Seminars Training
  • The Forum
  • Landmark Education
  • Benchmark Courses

A 1989 study concluded that LGATs had very little long-term effect on participants and that many of the marketing claims used by these organizations are unsubstantiated.
