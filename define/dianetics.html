Dianetics

Dianetics is a set of pseudo-psychiatric ideas about mental health and treatments for mental illnesses. The book is called "Dianetics: The Modern Science of Mental Health" and was written by L. Ron Hubbard and published in May 1950.

In the book, Hubbard introduces concepts called "the reactive mind", "the somatic mind" and "the analytical mind". He claims that "auditing" can be used to boost IQ and improve mental health.

These ideas have been criticized by medical professionals due to the lack of scientific content and outright pseudo-science.
